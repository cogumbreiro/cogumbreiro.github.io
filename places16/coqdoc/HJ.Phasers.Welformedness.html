Library HJ.Phasers.Welformedness



WellFormedness catpures a local property of taskviews: the relationship between signal-phase, wait-phase, and mode. It is an invariant of execution, thus, as we show, welformedness is preserved by the various reduction relations.
We, first, define the notion of welformed for taskviews.

Module Taskview.

A welformed taskview has three possible cases: (i) the task has wait-capability and is ready to issue a signal, in which case the signal-phase and wait-phase match; (ii) the task has wait-capability and has issued a signal, in which case the signal-phase is ahead of the wait-phase exactly one phase; (iii) the task is registered in signal-only mode, in which case the wait-phase cannot be ahead of the signal-phase.

  Inductive WellFormed v : Prop :=
    | tv_welformed_sw_eq:
      mode v = SIGNAL_WAIT ->
      wait_phase v = signal_phase v ->
      WellFormed v
    | tv_welformed_sw_succ:
      mode v = SIGNAL_WAIT ->
      S (wait_phase v) = signal_phase v ->
      WellFormed v
    | tv_welformed_so:
      mode v = SIGNAL_ONLY ->
      WellFormed v
    | tv_welformed_wo:
      mode v = WAIT_ONLY ->
      WellFormed v.

  Section Facts.
  Lemma tv_wellformed_inv_sw:
    forall v,
    WellFormed v ->
    mode v = SIGNAL_WAIT ->
    wait_phase v = signal_phase v \/ S (wait_phase v) = signal_phase v.

Regardless of the registration mode, the wait-phase cannot be greater than the signal-phase.

  Lemma welformed_wait_phase_le_signal_phase:
    forall v,
    WellFormed v ->
    mode v = SIGNAL_WAIT ->
    wait_phase v <= signal_phase v.

  Lemma tv_make_welformed:
    WellFormed Taskview.make.

  Lemma tv_signal_preserves_welformed:
    forall v,
    WellFormed v ->
    SignalPre v ->
    WellFormed (Taskview.signal v).

  Lemma tv_try_signal_preserves_welformed:
    forall v,
    WellFormed v ->
    WellFormed (Taskview.try_signal v).

  Lemma tv_wait_preserves_welformed:
    forall v,
    WellFormed v ->
    WaitPre v ->
    WellFormed (Taskview.wait v).

The operational semantics of taskviews preserves the property of WellFormed.

  Theorem tv_reduces_preserves_welformed:
    forall v o v´,
    WellFormed v ->
    Reduces v o v´ ->
    WellFormed v´.

  Lemma signal_phase_le_signal:
    forall v,
    WellFormed v ->
    signal_phase v <= signal_phase (signal v).

  Lemma reduces_wait_inv_wait_cap:
    forall v v´,
    WellFormed v ->
    mode v = SIGNAL_WAIT ->
    Reduces v WAIT v´ ->
    signal_phase v´ = wait_phase v´.

  Lemma reduces_trans_inv:
    forall x y z o,
    WellFormed x ->
    SignalCap (mode x) ->
    Reduces x WAIT y ->
    Reduces y o z ->
    o = SIGNAL.

  Let tv_wellformed_set_mode_sw:
    forall v r,
    WellFormed v ->
    mode v = SIGNAL_WAIT ->
    WellFormed (set_mode v r).

  Lemma set_mode_preserves_welformed:
    forall v r,
    WellFormed v ->
    r_le r (mode v) ->
    WellFormed (set_mode v r).

End Facts.
End Taskview.

A welformed phaser is such that every taskview mentioned in this phaser is welformed.

Module Phaser.

  Inductive WellFormed (ph:phaser) : Prop :=
    ph_welformed_def:
      (forall t v,
        Map_TID.MapsTo t v ph ->
        Taskview.WellFormed v) ->
      WellFormed ph.

  Lemma ph_welformed_add:
    forall t v ph,
    WellFormed ph ->
    Taskview.WellFormed v ->
    WellFormed (Map_TID.add t v ph).

  Lemma ph_welformed_to_tv_welformed:
    forall t v ph,
    WellFormed ph ->
    Map_TID.MapsTo t v ph ->
    Taskview.WellFormed v.

  Lemma ph_signal_preserves_welformed:
    forall ph t,
    WellFormed ph ->
    SignalPre t ph ->
    WellFormed (signal t ph).

  Lemma ph_try_signal_preserves_welformed:
    forall ph t,
    WellFormed ph ->
    WellFormed (try_signal t ph).

  Lemma ph_wait_preserves_welformed:
    forall ph t,
    WellFormed ph ->

  Lemma ph_try_wait_preserves_welformed:
    forall ph t,

  Lemma ph_drop_preserves_welformed:
    forall ph t,
    WellFormed ph ->

  Lemma ph_register_preserves_welformed:
    forall ph t r,

  Lemma ph_reduces_preserves_welformed:

  Lemma ph_make_welformed:
    forall t,
    WellFormed (make t).

End Phaser.

Nonsurprisingly, a welformed phasermap is such that every phaser mentioned in this phasermap is welformed.
