Tiago is an assistant professor at UMass Boston who researches data-driven techniques to write safer code at scale, by developing debugging tools, proving software correctness, and learning specifications.

Tiago's expertise include: improving the quality assurance of parallel programs by mitigating deadlocks & data-races, finding bugs in the wild by leveraging machine-learning to mine specifications.

Tiago is looking for self-motivated Ph.D. students who are interested in improving the quality of our software.
